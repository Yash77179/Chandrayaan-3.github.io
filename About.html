Chandrayaan-3: Exploring New Frontiers

Welcome to our website, where you can find information about Chandrayaan-3, India's third lunar exploration mission. Chandrayaan-3 consists of a lander and a rover, which are designed to safely land and explore the lunar south pole region, where large amounts of ice are believed to exist. Chandrayaan-3 was launched on 14 July 2023 and successfully landed on the moon on 23 August 2023, making India the fourth country to achieve this feat. On this website, you can learn more about the mission objectives, the scientific instruments, the landing site, and the data collected by the lander and the rover. We hope you enjoy this website and share your feedback with us. Thank you for your interest in Chandrayaan-3, India's pride and glory.
